Gajar ka Halwa

Description

A rich and delicious Indian dessert made with carrots, milk, and dry fruits.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg red carrots, grated
  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 4 tbsp ghee
  • 10-12 cashews, chopped
  • 10-12 almonds, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder

Steps

  1. Heat ghee in a heavy-bottomed pan
  2. Add grated carrots and saut√© for 5 minutes
  3. Add milk and cook on medium heat
  4. Stir occasionally until milk reduces completely
  5. Add sugar and mix well
  6. Cook until halwa thickens
  7. Add cardamom powder and dry fruits
  8. Cook for another 2-3 minutes
