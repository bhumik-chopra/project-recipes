Chole

Description

A spicy and tangy Punjabi chickpea curry best served with bhature.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpeas, soaked overnight
  • 1 tea bag (for authentic color)
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp chole masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp amchur (dry mango powder)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Pressure cook chickpeas with tea bag until soft
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds
  3. Add onions and sauté until golden
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté
  5. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates
  6. Add all spices and mix well
  7. Add cooked chickpeas with some water
  8. Simmer for 15-20 minutes
  9. Add amchur powder at the end
